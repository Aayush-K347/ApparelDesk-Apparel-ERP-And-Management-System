## ğŸ§  AI Commerce Intelligence & Smart Decisioning

This module transforms Luvarte from a traditional fashion storefront into a **proactive, AI-driven commerce engine**. By leveraging **user behavior analysis, collaborative filtering, and precision financial modeling**, the system enhances shopping decisions for users while enabling admins to evaluate profitability **before a transaction is finalized**.

### ğŸš€ Key Capabilities

#### 1. Personalized Recommendation Engine (The Brain)
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Uses historical order data, browsing activity, and interaction signals to understand user preferences
- **Collaborative Filtering (ALS)**: Applies matrix factorization techniques to identify latent product affinities across users
- **Personalized Suggestions**: Recommends products the user is statistically likely to purchase next

#### 2. AI Outfit Completion Engine (The Stylist)
- **Context Awareness**: Detects the base product added to cart (e.g., shirt, jacket)
- **Compatibility Logic**: Matches complementary items based on style, color palette, category, and past purchase patterns
- **Cross-Sell Optimization**: Automatically surfaces pants, shoes, and accessories to complete the outfit

#### 3. Virtual Try-On System (The Mirror)
- **Image-Based Try-On**: Allows users to upload a personal photo and preview selected apparel
- **Visual Confidence**: Helps users understand fit and appearance before purchase
- **Conversion Boost**: Reduces hesitation and potential returns by improving decision confidence

#### 4. Profit Simulation Engine (The Judge)
- **Precision Financial Waterfall**: Calculates net profit by deducting discounts, COGS, taxes, payment terms, and operational overhead
- **High-Accuracy Math**: Uses decimal-based arithmetic to avoid floating-point errors
- **Margin Evaluation**: Determines whether a transaction is healthy, risky, or loss-making

#### 5. AI Strategic Advisor (The Voice)
- **Decision Guidance**: Automatically labels transactions as APPROVE, CAUTION, or REJECT based on margin thresholds
- **Scenario Awareness**: Accounts for inventory levels to allow loss-leading or liquidation strategies when justified
- **Negotiation Support**: Provides breakeven discount limits for pricing discussions

#### 6. Scenario Comparison Dashboard
- **What-If Analysis**: Generates side-by-side views for no-discount, proposed, and breakeven scenarios
- **Visual Clarity**: Enables admins to instantly compare profitability outcomes
- **Actionable Insights**: Turns raw numbers into clear business decisions
